## Disc Burning: Every Format Covered

<a href="#">Toast Titanium Mac</a> disc burning covers every optical disc format in current use:

### Data Discs

Data disc burning creates a standard ISO 9660 or Mac HFS+ volume on CD, DVD, or Blu-ray.
<a href="#">Toast Mac</a> data disc features:

**Simple drag-and-drop**: Files and folders are dragged from Finder directly into the Toast
disc window. Toast calculates the disc size and displays whether the content fits on the
selected disc type.

**Disc spanning**: When content exceeds the capacity of a single disc, Toast automatically
spans the data across multiple discs, prompting for each disc at the appropriate time. Disc
spanning is essential for archiving large collections of files that exceed single-disc capacity.

**ISO image creation**: Create an ISO disc image from files without burning to physical media.
ISO images can be stored on hard drives or cloud storage and burned to disc later or mounted
virtually.

**ISO and disc image burning**: Burn any ISO, IMG, CDR, or BIN/CUE disc image file to physical
media with a single click.

**Disc duplication**: Copy an existing CD, DVD, or Blu-ray disc directly — insert the source
disc, eject, insert a blank, and Toast burns an exact copy. Multi-session, copy-protected
content exclusion, and direct disc-to-disc copy modes are supported.

### Audio CDs

<a href="#">Toast CD burner</a> for audio disc production:

**Red Book audio CD**: Create standard audio CDs from MP3, AAC, FLAC, WAV, AIFF, and other
audio formats. Toast converts the source audio to Red Book audio CD standard automatically
during the burn process.

**Gapless playback**: Configure gap duration between tracks from no gap (gapless, appropriate
for live recordings) to standard 2-second gaps. Gapless audio CDs play without audible silence
between tracks.

**CD-TEXT embedding**: Embed artist name, album name, and track title metadata in the disc's
CD-TEXT data so car stereos and CD players that support CD-TEXT display the track information.

**Track order and playlist editing**: Arrange tracks in any order by dragging within Toast's
track list. Preview each track before committing to the burn.

### Video DVDs with Menus

<a href="#">Roxio Toast Titanium</a> DVD production creates proper Video DVDs with interactive
menus:

**Video import**: Import MOV, MP4, AVI, MKV, and other video formats. Toast converts them
to MPEG-2 DVD video format during the burn process, handling the encoding automatically.

**Menu templates**: Choose from included disc menu templates that provide a chapter selection
interface. Menu templates include background graphics, typography, and navigation button styling.

**Chapter markers**: Set chapter markers at any point in the video for chapter navigation
from the disc menu.

**Auto-fit encoding**: Toast automatically adjusts the video encoding bitrate to fit the
content onto the disc at the highest quality possible, maximizing video quality within
the disc capacity.

### Blu-ray Discs

<a href="#">Toast 20 Titanium</a> Blu-ray burning:

**BD-R and BD-RE**: Burn to recordable Blu-ray (BD-R for write-once, BD-RE for rewritable).
Requires an external Blu-ray burner connected to the Mac — Macs do not include internal
Blu-ray drives.

**Video Blu-ray**: Create BDMV-format video Blu-ray discs with full HD and 4K content from
H.264, H.265, and other source formats, with navigation menus and chapter support.

**Data Blu-ray**: High-capacity data archival on 25GB (single layer), 50GB (dual layer),
and 100GB (BDXL) Blu-ray discs.

## Video Conversion

<a href="#">Roxio Toast Mac</a> video conversion handles the format transformation tasks that
complement disc burning:

**Format conversion**: Convert between MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, WMV, and dozens of other video
formats. Format conversion is needed when source files use codecs or containers incompatible
with the destination playback device or platform.

**Device presets**: Pre-configured conversion presets for Apple TV, iPhone, iPad, Android
devices, and other common playback targets encode at the correct resolution, bitrate, and
codec for each device.

**Batch conversion**: Convert multiple files simultaneously through the batch queue, running
conversion jobs in sequence or in parallel depending on system resources.

**Video editing before conversion**: Trim start and end points, merge multiple clips, and
apply basic color and audio adjustments before converting.

## Streaming Video Capture

<a href="#">Toast 20 Titanium</a> streaming video capture records video playing in the browser
or any Mac application as a video file:

**Screen capture**: Record the Mac screen as it plays streaming video, online content, or
any other video source. The capture produces a standard video file that can be stored locally
or burned to disc.

**Audio capture**: Capture audio from any Mac application — streaming music, web video audio,
or system audio — as a separate audio recording for later editing or burning to audio CD.

## Audio Ripping and Conversion

<a href="#">Roxio Toast software</a> audio tools provide CD ripping alongside the disc
burning capability:

**CD ripping**: Insert an audio CD and rip it to MP3, AAC, FLAC, AIFF, or WAV on the Mac.
Toast connects to online music databases to retrieve track metadata automatically.

**Vinyl and tape capture**: Connect a turntable or cassette deck to the Mac's audio input
and capture analog audio as a digital recording. Toast's capture tool records the analog
source and can split recordings at silence points to create individual track files.
